About our Company

We welcome you to discover four facets of Kazakhstan's tourism - the 4 E's: Eco, Ethnic, Entertainment and Events. Experience untouched nature and magnificent landscapes, unwind in authentic nomadic culture and gastronomy, try numerous entertainment options and enjoy vibrant festival and events of Kazakhstan". Kazakhstan.travel is an official destination website of Kazakhstan, where you can find motivating articles and useful information for travelers. Browse through «What to do» section to find out about interesting travel options, events, and tours. You can explore each province or city in detail by visiting «Where to go» section and find information about getting a Kazakhstan visa, as well as information about languages, time zones, currency, etc. in ‘Plan your trip’ section.Guides are sorted by language skills and specializations. Choose your travel guide by studying their profiles and photos from tours! Also you can contact a reliable tour operator, or find locations of gift shops through the website. Kazakhstan offers many tourist attractions and routes for every taste! Kazakhstan travel service has no analogues. We work every day to maintain the convenience and functionality of the website, regularly post photos, videos and new routes. Join us to update our database and tell about your business.
